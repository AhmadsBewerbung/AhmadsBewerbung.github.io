1. Overall Game Concept

Summary

FRONTRANGE is a 100-player sci-fi battle royale set on Astraeon Prime, a world fractured by unstable dimensional energy. The game revolves around mobility, adaptive weapons, and terrain-shaping technology instead of traditional building.

Visual Style

A mix of neon sci-fi + alien-natural environments. Semi-stylized characters with clean silhouettes and vivid environmental effects.

Unique Selling Points

  • Terrain manipulation through **GeoWeavers**.
  • Dynamic **Reality Fractures** altering physics mid-match.
  • Weapons that **adapt to your behavior**.
  • Seasonal **map rewrites**, not just cosmetic changes.

3. Core Gameplay Mechanics

Drop-In System

Players deploy from the Astraeon Ark with full control over drop speed, drift, and landing skill.

Loot System

High-tier items spawn in dangerous or rotating anomaly zones.

Weapon Rarity

  • Common
  • Advanced
  • Special
  • Prototype
  • Exotic

Inventory

6 main slots + grenade slot + GeoWeaver slot.

4. Movement & Controls

  • Sprinting, sliding, wall-running (short bursts).
  • Climbing & mantling.
  • Hoverboards, sand skimmers, grav-bikes, amphibious skiffs.
  • Reality surfing: momentum-based gliding inside fractures.

Purely cosmetic skins. No gameplay advantages.

7. Progression & Rewards

  • Leveling system
  • Season Pass (cosmetics only)
  • Challenges & Quests
  • Currency: Flux Shards

8. Sound & Music

A blend of ambient sci-fi pads + rhythmic percussion during combat. Sound cues are spatial and dynamically react to anomalies.

9. Technical Details

  • Recommended Engine: Unreal Engine 5
  • Nanite + Lumen for detailed sci-fi worlds
  • Optimized for 100-player servers
  • Cross-platform support

10. Monetization

100% fair. No pay-to-win. Cosmetics only.

  • Skins
  • Gliders
  • Emotes
  • Season Pass

11. Story & Lore

Astraeon Prime was shattered by a cosmic event known as “The Sundering.” Factions now battle for control of Rift Energy and the lost technologies beneath the surface.

12. Example Match Flow

  • Queue → Deploy → Loot → Fight → Rotate → Final Circle → Victory Pulse
